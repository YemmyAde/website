  • PROPHECY OF THE CHURCH ESTABLISHMENT

  • FULFILLMENT OF PROPHECY

  • INAUGURAL SERVICE

The book of Proverbs 29:18 says where there is no “vision”, the people perish. We thank God that long before the establishment of the Church, there were series of revelations; and revelations are still coming concerning the mission of the Church and where the Lord is taking the Church to.

Among the early revelations was the one from Snr. Apos. Prophet D. S. Jolayemi during a Saturday Watch-night Service at C & S Movement Church, No 4, Ilorin on 31st January, 1987 when he revealed that God would transfer then Snr. Apostle A. A. Bodunrin to Abuja for a special purpose with respect to Church coordination.

In May 1988, another revelation was made by Prophet J. I. O. Abiose (now Special Apostle) at C & S Movement Church, Sanusi Street, Kano where he prophesied that the General Leader and the Chairman of Surulere District, Special Apos. Prophet (Dr.) G. O. Fakeye (of the blessed memory) will establish a Branch of the Church at Abuja through Special Apos. A. A. Bodunrin.

It was not until January 1990 when events began to unfold. The Chairman of the Central Planning Implementation Committee (CPIC) of C&S Movement Church Worldwide, Overseer of Abuja Area and Special Apostle for Evangelism, Special Apostle Prophet (Dr.) G. O. Fakeye on request of His Grace Baba Aladura, Pastor J. T. Agboola (of the blessed memory) toured the Federal Capital Territory (FCT) with some Church Elders; and they submitted their findings to then Baba Aladura at the National Headquarters, Kaduna.

Their findings revealed that other Christian Denominations visited in Abuja during their tour had many branches within FCT and noted with dismay that our sect; “C&S Movement Church” had just one Branch within Abuja.

Based on this report, the then Baba Aladura, Late Pastor J. T. Agboola directed that more lands should be acquired by this denomination for the expansion of the Church in FCT, so that the gospel (word of God) will be propagated to win more souls for Christ and also to serve all new members and new converts all over FCT.

This directive led to the establishment of the Church. On 12th August, 1990, the establishment of C&S Church Movement, Surulere Abuja Branch was announced for the first time during the Anniversary Celebration of St. Peter’s Band of C&S Movement Church, Surulere, Lagos, the Band of Special Apostle A. A. Bodunrin; and he was also pronounced the Leader- in-Charge of the newly established Church.

After the pronouncement of this Church, Special Apostle Prophet S. O. Aiyegbusi, a member of Surulere Council of Elders and Patron of Daniel Band (now the Amazing Grace District Chairman/General Leader) played prominent roles by constantly visiting Abuja and worshipping with the Branch, and guiding the Church through series of revelations and spiritual advice.

Likewise, Special Apos. Prophet G. O. Jagun (of the blessed memory) who was the Alternate General Secretary of C&S Movement Church Surulere Distict in Lagos played active roles by worshipping in this Church whenever he visits Abuja and guiding the Church through series of revelations. Other Prophets that visited the Church at its inception from our Surulere District mother Church included Late M/S/Apos.

Prophet G. O. Adeshina, Late M/S/Apos Prophet Olojo, etc. The objective of the Church establishment, according to God as was revealed on February 17, 1991 by Evang. K. Alamutu (now in Lagos) was on “TRUTH”. That is, the Church should be truthful in all her ways through the propagation of the “true word of God” to the lost souls and members of the fold worldwide as indicated in John 8:32, “And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall set you free”.

It was further revealed that the glory of the Church will be seen worldwide. The Church Tenet of Faith was mainly “Believe in ONE GOD, ONE FAITH and LOVE the LORD GOD with all your heart, soul and might”; and secondly, to love all our neighbors as ourselves.

The directive from Baba Aladura to establish Church branches in Abuja brought to pass all these revelations as well as the spiritual guidance presented before the First Supervising Prophet of C&S Movement Church Worldwide, Special Apostle E. O. Akinrele (of the blessed memory) at M/S/Apos.

Alogbo’s house at Abule-Oja concerning Surulere District Headquarters, Lagos. The revelation “perceives Surulere District, Lagos as a big tree with MANY BRANCHES BEARING FRUITS TOGETHER with many birds flying in the sky and numerous people eating out of these fruits.

The tree serves as an umbrella-giving shed over these people with the air of comfort and contentment blowing over them. This means that Surulere District with her Branches (including Dakibiyu District) will be great and that God would allow all and sundry to see this glory. As the tree is tall, so will the Church be great.”

We therefore, thank God once again for the fulfillment of all His prophecies and promises and for making the Church that was formerly a Branch under Surulere District to be upgraded to Surulere Circuit Headquarters; and through its’ landmark progress and achievements and on the recommendation made by the Surulere District Chairman/General Leader, Special Apostle G. O. Fakeye to Baba Aladura, became a District i.e. Dakibiyu District Headquarters on 18th June 2006.

The Holy Order of Cherubim and Seraphim Movement Church Worldwide, Dakibiyu District Headquarters, Dakibiyu, Abuja had her inaugural service on Sunday 2nd September, 1990 at No 5, Flat 1, Naomi Street, Zone 2, Wuse, Abuja (popularly known as Block 406), then residence of S/M/I/I Ajoke Oluwafunmilayo Adegoke nee Oyelola (of blessed memory) with eleven (11) members in attendance.

The members were Evang. O. A. Adegoke (now Special Apostle and Deputy District Chairman) as the Worship Leader and Snr. Apos. A. A. Bodunrin, the Leader in Charge (now Special Apostle and the District Chairman/General Leader) as the Preacher.

Others were M/S/M/I/I J. B. Oyelola (of blessed memory), Bro Tokunbo Emmanuel Adegoke (now Apostle and in USA), Evang. Adedayo Bodunrin, Teach. Oluwatosin Adegoke, Teach. Kayode Bodunrin, Bro. Mayowa Oyelola (now in Lagos), Sist. Sherifat Daramola (now in Ijebu Ode), Sist. Ifeoluwapo Egweni, and Sist. Opeyemi Bodunrin.
