ABOUT

The Taku Ti Jesu Band

The promises of God for those who serve him in truth and indeed are numerous. In spite of God’s benevolence, the world we live in is full of challenges, hatred, violence, injustices, etc., most of which are ruled by Satan and its demonic forces. Indeed, God did not promise his elect immunity from such forces of darkness, BUT God promised that his elect will always overcome In view of the vicissitudes of life which the children of God are always exposed to.

CSMC Dakibiyu District Headquarters established the TAKU TI JESU programme, which literarily means HOLD/CLING ON TO JESUS The programme commenced in March 2018 and it takes place every third Saturday of every month between 7.00 am and 9.00 am. It is open to members and visitors. Interestingly, the attendance of visitors seeking solutions to their challenges of life has been very overwhelming. God has been doing wonders because of the overflow of his mercies as evidenced in the avalanche of Testimonies of God’s intervention, blessings and mercies.

The programme gives room for Praise Worship/Thanksgiving, Testimonies, Exhortation and Divine Revelations. It also includes comprehensive Prayer sessions for Mercy, Blessings, Favour, Protection, Victory, Deliverance, personal requests, etc. God has been so wonderful and merciful for flattening all mountains of challenges, levelling up all valleys of trepidation/unhappiness, straightening up all crooked ways for all that care to come under his banner; and giving succour to troubled souls. To God be the Glory!!!
