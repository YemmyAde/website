CHOIR BAND HOLDS ITS 29TH YEAR ANNIVERSARY THANKSGIVING SERVICE




The 29th CHOIR BAND Anniversary of the C&S MOVEMENT CHURCH, DAKIBIYU DISTRICT HEADQUARTERS, ABUJA was held on Sunday 18th April 2021 at the Church Auditorium. It was a season of giving praises and adoration to God Almighty for several reasons which included:

God's protection and sustenance of members and their activities especially during the COVID 19 pandemic and the attendant National socio-economic challenges

In 2021 the annual Anniversary was celebrated through the social media because of the COVID 19 pandemic. But this year, God made it possible for members to physically and gloriously worship and praise God Almighty for his benevolence & grace

It pleased God to ensure that every member was safe and no soul was lost. Glory be to God.

The pre-event activity was marked by mounting series of prayers which culminated in the glorious Thanksgiving Service. The Preacher of the day was Special Apostle Prophet Ejalonibu who delivered God's powerful message on GIVING IS LIVING with text taken from Matthew 25:31-46 and Prov. 3:1-10

DAKIBIYU DISTRICT HOLDS PASTOR'S COUNCIL RETREAT/SEMINAR




As part of its Capacity Building and Development programme, the Dakibiyu District Pastors Council organised and executed a Retreat/Seminar for ordained Pastors and Pastors-in-training in the District. The event took place on 27 February 2021 at the Church Auditorium

The one-day programme featured two well researched Papers presented by erudite experts in the field. This was followed by a rewarding Questions/Answer/Comments session. The first Paper titled PASTORAL ANOINTING was delivered by Sp. M/I/I Pastor Dr. D. I. OGBEDE, the District Supervising Pastor

The second Paper titled THE PASTOR AND THE CHURCH was delivered by the guest Lecturer, Rev. Prof. Bamidele AKINLOLU, the Provost, Pan African Bible Seminary & College of Clergies & Management Studies.

DAKIBIYU DISTRICT CELEBRATES 30 YEARS ANNIVERSARY




The Dakibiyu District of the Cherubim & Seraphim Church Movement Worldwide, Abuja celebrated its 30th Year Anniversary between Saturday 31 October 2020 to 1 November 2020 at its Church Auditorium in Abuja. It was a twin celebration as the occasion was used to mark the 2020 Harvest Thanksgiving of the Church. In spite of all the prevailing COVID 19 challenges, the event was a huge success. Glory be to God!

The 30 Year Anniversary which had its Theme on CELEBRATING GOD'S STEADFAST LOVE kicked off on Saturday 31 October 2020 with a Lecture titled: UNDERSTANDING GOD'S STEADFAST LOVE: GOD, GREED & THE GOSPEL IN OUR CONTEMPORARY GENERATION. The Lecture was delivered by the Guest Lecturer, His Eminence John Cardinal Onaiyekan, Archbishop Emeritus, Catholic Diocese of Abuja. This was followed by an Award Ceremony, during which awards were presented to individuals and organizations that made significant contributions to the success story of CSMC, Dakibiyu District from inception to date.

The Thanksgiving Service for the 30 Year Anniversary and the 2020 Harvest of Dakibiyu District Headquarters took place on 1 November 2020 at the Church Auditorium. The Preacher was Rev. Prof. Bamidele S. Akinlolu, the Provost of PAN African Bible Seminary & College of Clergies & Management Studies, Nigeria. The event was a grand success. Glory be to God.

May God's Steadfast Love continue to abide with us, the Church and Nigeria in Jesus Mighty Name Amen.
