ABOUT

Shiloh Group

The Shiloh Group was established in response to the needs of people (both members and non-members) seeking solutions to the myriad of life’s challenges faced on a daily basis. Some of the challenges include seeking for God’s favour, Mercy, Divine Help in undertakings, Victory and Deliverance, Healing of the Sick, Success, provision of Spouses, seeking for Divine Direction, etc. It is indeed what can be termed Spiritual Clinic for the Needy.

The Shiloh service, which comes up on every Wednesday between the hours of 9.00 am and 12.00 noon, is chacterised by Praise Worship/Thanksgiving, Testimonies, Spiritual Clinic, Exhortation, Prayer session and Divine Revelations. Though open to both members and visitors, the attendance by visitors are always overwhelming. Persons from all works of life and different professionals including artisans, traders, etc. with different challenges are always in attendance.

God is always merciful for answered prayers as attested by the several Testimonies. It is therefore no wonder that the maxim of the Shiloh Group goes thus, In Shiloh, Prayers are Answered
