The Warden Band

Church wardens are officials with special supervisory duties on administrative, finance, social and spiritual activities; and who ensure that all Church regulations and laws are observed. The office of Chief Warden or a Chamberlain which is the Captain of the guard was at all times one of great dignity and responsibility (I kings 14:28, Neh. 4:22).In Cherubim and Seraphim Movement Church, there are five divisions in Church Warden department, namely:

  1. Altar Wardens: They are in charge of the high and working Altars. They end up getting promoted to Church Stewards after Divine clarification from God.
  2. People’s Warden: They take care of the people in the main worship Hall during and after Church services.
  3. Gate Warden: They are always on guard in all the Church entrances during services to take care of members and all other people who come for worship.
  4. Leader’s Warden or Curate: These Wardens take care of the Church Elders at the inner Altars during services and their movements. They can become Church Properties Stewards.
  5. Church Steward: Stewardship begins in the Bible history when Eliezer was Abraham’s house-keeper (Gen. 15:2). Joseph’s stewardship was when he was a Governor of Egypt (Gen. 43:19).

In New Testament terms, Jesus Christ described the Kingdom of Heaven as the workers in the vineyard of their masters (Matt. 20:1-16). As far as Christians are concerned, stewardship involves responsibility of managing God’s work through the Church. God has appointed all Christians to be His stewards on earth. In Cherubim and Seraphim Church, you must use your talents to work conscientiously in the house of God, and win more souls for Christ.
